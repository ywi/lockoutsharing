## üéØ What is Lockout Sharing?  
[Lockout Sharing](https://wowpedia.fandom.com/wiki/Raid_lockout) in **World of Warcraft** allows multiple characters or players to benefit from the same raid lockout.  
A player with a saved lockout can invite others to their group. I share near 24/7 lockouts daily for faster mount farming.<br>
<br>
By simply whispering Ywishare-Illidan `!id` You access retail-EU lockouts (instance list available below) , if you're the oposite faction (Alliance) you should join a Ywishare communitiy to be able to whisper. 

### üîπ Example:  
If the 9th boss drops a mount, I invite you to a lockout where the first 8 bosses are already killed‚Äîyou join and directly kill the 9th boss.  
‚úÖ The host does not need to be present, making the process automatic.

It is also where you can track if Ywishare is online through the member list.

To join a community:

1. Open the **Guilds & Communities** tab (default key: `J`)
2. Click **"Create or Join Community"**
3. Enter either of the following invite codes:  `2ZrL7VbSoWp` - `rjR7x9eULA2` - `GGjjWk4Hxzj` - `wWBAxJEcZeg`

## üöÄ Requesting a Lockout

1. Make sure you're **not in a party** and standing **outside the instance** you want to join.
2. Whisper `/w Ywishare-Illidan !id` to receive a lockout.
3. Follow the instructions provided in chat.

### üö¶ Before Entering the Instance 

- **Normal Mode:** `!normal` or `!nhc` (default setting if Normal & HC are available)  
- **Heroic Mode:** `!heroic` or `!hc`

### üë• Inviting Friends

- You can simply suggest invite your friends  or alternatively type `!lead`  before entering the instance ‚Äî you‚Äôll then have **30 seconds** to list your group in lfg to invite others. (Raid: Legacy permits cross-faction listings)

### üîç Optional Commands

- `!q` or `!queue` To check your current position in the queue.
- `!leave` Removes you from the queue.
-  `!plus` Support hosting Ywishare and access priority queue.
